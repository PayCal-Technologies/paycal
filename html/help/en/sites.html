Sites Management Help

Overview of Sites

In PayCal.app, sites represent the different locations or projects where you work. Each site has its own settings for pay rates, allowances, and other details. Properly managing your sites ensures accurate earnings calculations when logging work entries on the Calendar page.

How to Manage Sites

To access site management:

  1. Navigate to the Sites section from the main menu.
  2. You'll see a list of your existing sites.

Adding a New Site

  1. Click the "Add Site" button.
  2. Fill in the details:
    • Site Name: A descriptive name for the site (e.g., "Office HQ", "Remote Project A").
    • Wage Rate: Your hourly pay rate for this site.
    • Living Out Allowance (LOA): The daily allowance amount if applicable.
    • Travel Time Rate: Compensation per hour for travel time related to this site.
    • Notes: Optional additional information.
  3. Click "Save" to add the site.

Editing an Existing Site

  1. Select the site from the list.
  2. Click "Edit" or directly modify the fields.
  3. Update any details as needed.
  4. Click "Save" to apply changes.

Deleting a Site

  1. Select the site you want to remove.
  2. Click "Delete" and confirm the action.
  3. Note: Deleting a site will not remove associated work entries but may affect future calculations if entries reference it.

Why Sites Matter for Work Entries

When you enter a work entry on the Calendar page, you select a site. This links the entry to the site's specific pay details:

  • Ensures the correct wage is applied to your hours.
  • Calculates LOA and travel time accurately.
  • Allows for different rates across various locations or projects.

For example, a construction site might have higher travel time and LOA compared to an office site.
