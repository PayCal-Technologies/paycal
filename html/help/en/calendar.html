Calendar Page Help

Overview of the Calendar Page

The main Calendar page is the central hub for tracking your work hours in PayCal.app. It displays an interactive calendar view where you can:

  • View your work entries for specific dates or periods.
  • Add new work entries for days worked.
  • Edit existing entries to correct or update information.
  • Navigate between months and years to review historical data.
  • Generate reports and summaries of your earnings and hours.

The calendar highlights days with entries and provides quick access to detailed views for each day.

How to Enter a Work Entry

To log your work hours:

  1. Navigate to the Calendar page from the main menu.
  2. Select the date you want to add an entry for by clicking on it in the calendar view.
  3. In the entry form that appears, fill in the following details:
    • Site: Choose the work site where you worked (see relationship to sites below).
    • Start Time: Enter the time you started work.
    • End Time: Enter the time you finished work.
    • Break Time: If applicable, specify any unpaid break time.
    • Overtime: Indicate if this is overtime hours.
    • Living Out Allowance (LOA): Check if you're eligible for LOA on this day.
    • Travel Time: Add any travel time related to the job.
    • Notes: Optional notes for additional details.
  4. Click "Save" to record the entry. The calendar will update to reflect the new entry.

You can edit existing entries by clicking on the date and modifying the form.

Relationship Between Work Entries and Sites

Each work entry is associated with a specific work site. Sites define important details such as:

  • Hourly wage rates.
  • Living out allowance amounts.
  • Travel time compensation.

This association ensures accurate earnings calculations, as different sites may have different pay structures. For example, working at a remote site might include travel time and LOA, while office work might not.

To manage your sites, including adding new ones or updating details, refer to the Sites Management Help Page. It's essential to set up your sites correctly before entering work entries to ensure precise tracking and calculations.
