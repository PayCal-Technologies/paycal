Earnings Help

Overview of Earnings

In PayCal.app, the Earnings section provides a comprehensive view of your work hours, pay calculations, and financial summaries. It helps you track earnings across different periods, understand deductions, and export data for payroll or records.

Viewing Earnings Reports

To access your earnings:

  1. Log in and navigate to the Earnings tab from the main menu.
  2. Choose a view: Daily, Monthly, or Yearly reports.
  3. Select a specific date range or period to filter results.

The dashboard displays:

  • Total Hours: Regular, overtime, and any special hours (e.g., travel or living out allowance).
  • Gross Pay: Your earnings before deductions.
  • Deductions: Taxes, EI, CPP, and other withholdings.
  • Net Pay: Your take-home amount.

Understanding Calculations

PayCal.app calculates earnings based on:

  • Site Rates: Hourly wage, overtime multipliers, and allowances set in your sites.
  • Pay Periods: Weekly or bi-weekly cycles, as configured in settings.
  • Tax Rules: Automatic calculations for federal/provincial taxes, EI, and CPP based on your location and income.

For example, overtime is calculated at 1.5x your regular rate after 40 hours in a week. Ensure your site details are accurate for precise calculations.

Exporting Data

You can export earnings data in various formats:

  1. Go to the Earnings view and select "Export".
  2. Choose format: CSV (for spreadsheets), PDF (for printing), or TXT (plain text).
  3. Specify the date range and download the file.

This is useful for submitting to payroll, tax preparation, or personal records.

Troubleshooting Earnings Issues

If calculations seem incorrect:

  • Verify work entries for accuracy in the Calendar.
  • Check site settings in Sites Management.
  • Ensure your pay period and tax info are up to date in Settings.
  • Contact support if issues persist.
